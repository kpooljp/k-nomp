Getting Started
  • Download kotod
  • Download cpu miner
  • ./minerd -a yescrypt -o stratum+tcp://jp.kpool.jp:3333 -u [koto's wallet address]
  • Download gpu miner
  • ./ccminer -a yescrypt -o stratum+tcp://jp.kpool.jp:3333 -u [koto's wallet address]
